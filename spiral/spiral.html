Drawing a spiral

To generate the spiral image, we'll use three parameters, including:

  • Iterations: determines the number of segments to be drawn in the spiral
  • Angle: controls the way each segment is drawn, determining the direction and tilt of the spiral.
  • Step: Allows modification of the segment length in each iteration.
